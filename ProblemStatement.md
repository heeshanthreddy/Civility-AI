# **Civility.ai – Content Moderation System**

## **1. Problem Statement**
Social media platforms and community applications allow users to upload content such as images, videos, and comments. Without proper moderation, harmful, abusive, or inappropriate content can be published, which may negatively impact users and violate platform policies.

The goal of this project is to develop an AI-powered system that automatically reviews user-generated content before it is published and ensures that only safe and appropriate content appears on the platform.

## **2. Requirement**
Civility.ai is an intelligent moderation system that analyzes user-generated images, videos, and comments in real time. The system detects harmful, violent, abusive, or irrelevant content and prevents it from being published.

The system helps maintain a safe digital environment by automatically allowing safe content and flagging unsafe content for review or rejection.

## **3. Key Features**
- Automatic moderation of images, videos, and text comments  
- Detection of harmful, abusive, or inappropriate content  
- Real-time content validation before publishing  
- AI-powered analysis for better accuracy  
- Supports multiple types of user-generated content  
- Helps maintain a safe and respectful online community  

## **4. How the System Works**
1. Users upload content such as an image, video, or comment.  
2. The system analyzes the content using AI models.  
3. Harmful or unsafe patterns are detected.  
4. Safe content is approved automatically.  
5. Unsafe content is blocked or flagged for review.
